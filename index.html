LOFRIX ECO GEL is an exciting and easy-to-apply technology that will dramatically reduce your car’s harmful emissions, save you fuel, and protect your engine. LOFRIX ECO GEL has been proven, by extensive independent testing with Government-approved MOT equipment, to slash those deadly exhaust emissions that pollute the air in our towns, cities and countryside and create a health hazard for all of us. Our product family has more than 18,000,000 hours of successful, failure-free use in a variety of industries, and has solved some of the most challenging engineering problems along the way.

LOFRIX ECO GEL is our most recent product, intended primarily for use in motor vehicles, a huge source of toxic emissions.
